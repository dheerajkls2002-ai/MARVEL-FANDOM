⚡ WHAT IS MARVEL?

UNIVERSE TYPE: SUPERHERO & VILLAINS WORLD — A FICTIONAL UNIVERSE WITH THOUSANDS OF CHARACTERS ACROSS COMICS, FILMS, AND TV SHOWS.

MAIN FOCUS: HEROES SAVING THE WORLD FROM THREATS BOTH COSMIC AND EARTHLY.

CREATION YEAR: 1939 — FOUNDED AS TIMELY COMICS, LATER BECAME MARVEL COMICS IN 1961.

FAMOUS TEAMS: AVENGERS, X-MEN, GUARDIANS OF THE GALAXY, FANTASTIC FOUR, S.H.I.E.L.D.

MAIN THEME: POWER, SACRIFICE, HEROISM, RESPONSIBILITY, AND THE COST OF GREATNESS.

WHY PEOPLE LOVE MARVEL: EPIC STORYTELLING, EMOTIONAL CHARACTER ARCS, HUMOUR, ACTION, AND UNIVERSE-BUILDING UNLIKE ANY OTHER.

IMMERSION: STEP INTO THE WORLD OF HEROES, VILLAINS, POWERS, AND EPIC BATTLES SPANNING GALAXIES AND DIMENSIONS.

JOURNEY: FROM STEVE ROGERS BECOMING CAPTAIN AMERICA TO TONY STARK SACRIFICING HIMSELF — EVERY HERO HAS AN UNFORGETTABLE JOURNEY.

VILLAINS: DISCOVER POWERFUL ENEMIES LIKE THANOS, HELA, ULTRON, LOKI, GREEN GOBLIN, AND DOCTOR DOOM.

MULTIVERSE: MARVEL NOW EXPLORES INFINITE ALTERNATE REALITIES — EVERY CHOICE CREATES A NEW UNIVERSE.

ICONIC MOMENTS: "I AM IRON MAN", CAP LIFTING MJOLNIR, SPIDER-MAN'S NO WAY HOME REUNION — MOMENTS THAT DEFINED A GENERATION.
